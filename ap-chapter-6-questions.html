Osteogenic cells - what is its function?

These cells are involved in the formation of new bone and are found in the growing parts of bone, including the periosteum and endosteum.

Osteoblasts - what is its function?

These are the primary cells that form bone and secrete matrix materials, including collagen.

Osteoclasts - what is its function?

These cells are responsible for the breakdown and resorption of bone tissue.

Osteocytes - what is its function?

These are mature bone cells that maintain the bone matrix and communicate with other cells.
