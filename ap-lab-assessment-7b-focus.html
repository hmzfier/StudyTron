Other

Action: Flex and rotate head

Origin: Manubrium, Clavicle

Insertion: Temporal

Muscles that move the scapula = rhomboid major/minor, pectoralis minor, serratus anterior, trapezius

Action: Protracts if FRONT muscle, Retracts If BACK muscle

Origin: Ribs

Insertion: Scapula

Arms

Deltoid

Action: Abducts arm

Origin: Scapula

Insertion: Humerus

Shape: Convergent

pectoralis major

Action: Flexes, Adducts arm

Origin: Clavicle, sternum, costal cartilages 1-7

Insertion: Humerus

Shape: convergent

latissimus dorsi

Action: Adducts, Extends, arm

Origin: Iliac crest of pelvic, lumbar vertebrae

Insertion: Humerus

Shape: Convergent

biceps brachii

Action: Supinates, Flexes the forearm

Origin: Scapula

Insertion: Radius

Shape: Fusiform

triceps brachii

Action: Extends the forearm

Origin: Humerus, Scapula

Insertion: Ulna

Shape: Fusiform

Muscles that Move the Hand

Action: Ulnaris and Radialis either extends or flexes hand based on front/back. They BOTH also abduct or adduct the hand based on lateral/medial.

Origin: Humerus

Insertion: Metacarpal

palmaris longus

Action: Flexes Hand

Origin: Humerus

Insertion: Flexor retinaculum and palmar aponeurosis

Shape: fusiform

pronator teres

Action: Pronates Forearm

Origin: Humerus, Ulna

Insertion: Radius

Shape: Fusiform

Legs

Origin: Sternum

Insertion: diaphragm

Gluteus maximus

Action: Abducts Hip, Extends Hip

Origin: Ilium

Insertion: Femur

Gracilis

Action: Adducts leg

Origin: Pubis

hamstrings

Hamstrings - biceps femoris, semitendinosus, semimembranosus

Action: Extends Hip, flexes knee

Origin: Ischial tuberosity*

Insertion: Tibia

Shape: Fusiform

Quadriceps femoris - rectus femoris, vastus intermedius, lateralis, medialis

Action: Extends Knee

Origin: Femur

Insertion: Tibial tuberosity

Shape: Unipennate

Lower leg Muscles that move the foot and toes

Summary

Action: Plantarflexes foot if located in posterior (back), dorsiflexes foot if anterior (front)

Origin: Femur

Insertion: Calcaneus

Shape: Fusiform
