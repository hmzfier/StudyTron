Names Tell You Actions

Adductor / Abductor Moves toward/away from midline

Flexor / Extensor Flexes or extends

Levator / Depressor Lifts / lowers

Pronator / Supinator Pronates / supinates forearm

Rotator Rotates bone

Hip / Thigh

Anterior thigh (Quadriceps) Extend the knee

Posterior thigh (Hamstrings) Flex the knee + Extend the hip

Medial thigh (Adductors) Adduct the thigh

Lateral hip (Glute med/min, TFL) Abduct the thigh

Arm/Shoulder

Anterior arm (biceps region) Flex the Forearm

Posterior arm (triceps region) Extend the Forearm

Anterior shoulder/chest (pec/ant. deltoid) Flex the arm at shoulder

Posterior shoulder/back (lats/post. deltoid) Extend the arm at shoulder

Origin/Insertion

Origins are usually on the stable bone; insertions are on the moving bone

Muscles often originate on the big, stable, or proximal structures

Hamstrings Ischial tuberosity

Quadriceps Femur (except rectus femoris: AIIS)

Adductors Pubis / ischium (medial pelvis)

Glutes Ilium (lateral pelvis)

Rotator cuff Scapula

Abdominal Muscles

Summary

Action: Flexes the trunk

Shape: All parallel except diaphragm which is convergent

External Oblique

Action: Flexes the trunk

Origin: Inferior 8 ribs

Insertion: Ilium

Shape: Parallel

Internal Oblique

Action: Flexes and laterally flexes the trunk

Origin: ilium

Insertion: Inferior 4 ribs

Shape: Parallel

Rectus Abdominis

Action: Flexes the trunk

Origin: Pubic bones

Insertion: Inferior Ribs

Shape: Parallel

Transversus Abdominis

Action: Compresses abdomen

Origin: Inferior 6 costal cartilages

Insertion: Linea alba

Shape: Parallel

Muscles of Ventilation

Summary

Origin: Upper ribs

Insertion: Lower rib except diaphragm which inserts on itself

Diaphragm

Action: Inspiration

Origin: Sternum

Insertion: diaphragm

Shape: Convergent

External Intercostals

Action: Elevate the rib cage, inspiration

Origin: Upper rib

Insertion: Lower rib

Shape: Parallel

Internal intercostals

Action: Depress the rib cage, expiration

Origin: Upper rib

Insertion: Lower rib

Shape: Parallel
