Abdominal Muscles

Summary

Action: Flexes the trunk

Shape: All parallel except diaphragm which is convergent

External Oblique

Action: Flexes the trunk

Origin: Inferior 8 ribs

Insertion: Ilium

Shape: Parallel

Internal Oblique

Action: Flexes and laterally flexes the trunk

Origin: ilium

Insertion: Inferior 4 ribs

Shape: Parallel

Rectus Abdominis

Action: Flexes the trunk

Origin: Pubic bones

Insertion: Inferior Ribs

Shape: Parallel

Transversus Abdominis

Action: Compresses abdomen

Origin: Inferior 6 costal cartilages

Insertion: Linea alba

Shape: Parallel

Muscles of Ventilation

Summary

Origin: Upper ribs

Origin: Ribs except diaphragm

Insertion: Lower ribcept diaphragm

Diaphragm

Action: Inspiration

Origin: Sternum

Insertion: diaphragm

Shape: Convergent

External Intercostals

Action: Elevate the rib cage, inspiration

Origin: Upper rib

Insertion: Lower rib

Shape: Parallel

Internal intercostals

Action: Depress the rib cage, expiration

Origin: Upper rib

Insertion: Lower rib

Shape: Parallel
