What stimulates osteoblasts and bone deposition

What does parathyroid hormone do in bone remodeling?

makes sure your blood always has enough calcium by pulling it from bone when needed.

What are the two main classifications of bone fractures?

Simple (closed) -bone breaks, skin and tissue around fracture remain intact

compound (open) - bone breaks, skin and tissues around fracture are damaged

Compound = higher risk of infection because the bone is exposed to the environment.

How does bone fracture healing begin?

Chondroblasts secrete cartilage to form a soft callus.

What happens after the soft callus forms during bone healing?

Osteoblasts replace the soft callus with a bone callus made of primary bone.

Which hormone, produced by the thyroid gland, works opposite to parathyroid hormone (PTH)?

Calcitonin

Bone tissue is so strong and resists tensile forces because of the ___ fibers found in the extracellular matrix.

Collagen

The osteoclast can break down bone by secreting what 2 tools?

hydrogen ions (H+) and enzymes
