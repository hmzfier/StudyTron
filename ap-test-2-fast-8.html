Which muscles push food into the esophagus?

Pharyngeal constrictor muscles.

Which 2 muscles are involved in inspiration?

The diaphragm and external intercostal muscles.

Which muscles cause air to leave the lungs during forced expiration?

The internal intercostal muscles.

Which muscle is the agonist of arm abduction?

Deltoid muscle.

Which muscles extend the arm at the elbow joint?

Triceps brachii and anconeus.

Muscles with a triangular shape, such as the pectoralis major muscle in the chest, typically have a __________fascicle arrangement

convergent fascicle arrangement
