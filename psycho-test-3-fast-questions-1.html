Why do scientists believe that women get drunk on average quicker than men?

Lower alcohol dehydrogenase (ADH)

Women have lower alcohol dehydrogenase (ADH) activity in the stomach, so they break down less alcohol before it enters the bloodstream.

More unmetabolized alcohol reaches the blood → higher BAC → they get drunk faster.

Why is tolerance to barbiturates especially dangerous?

Because tolerance to barbiturates develops quickly for the “high” but not for the lethal respiratory-depressant effects.

That means the dose needed to feel effects goes up, but the dose that can stop breathing does not, making overdose very easy and very dangerous.
