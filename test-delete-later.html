Chapter 8 Questions

The 3 function of joints

Movement and stability and growth of bones

Synarthroses, Amphiarthroses, Diarthroses

Synarthroses: Immovable joints (e.g., sutures of the skull)

Amphiarthroses: Slightly movable joints (e.g., intervertebral discs, pubic symphysis)

Diarthroses: Freely movable joints (e.g., shoulder, hip)

What are fibrous joints made of?

Dense regular connective tissue

What do synovial joints have on the surface?

Hyaline cartilage

What covers the end of synovial joints?

Articular Cartilage
